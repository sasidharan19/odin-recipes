Veggie Supreme Spinach Nachos Recipe

This innovative twist on traditional nachos brings together the vibrant flavours of fresh spinach, a zesty salsa sauce, and a medley of perfectly boiled vegetables. Topped with a luscious, velvety cheese dip, these nachos are a true delight for both your taste buds and your health-conscious choices. Whether you're hosting a gathering with friends or simply looking to elevate your snacking experience, our Spinach Nachos with Salsa Sauce and Cheesy Dip are guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser, adding a burst of flavour and wholesome goodness to every bite. (Recipe and Image courtesy: Cornitos)

Incredients

  • 70 gm nachos
  • 20 gm black olives
  • 60 gm sour cream
  • 50 ml salsa sauce
  • salt as required
  • 45 gm boiled frozen sweet corn
  • 10 gm coriander leaves
  • 30 gm yellow bell pepper
  • 100 gm cheese dip
  • black pepper as required

How to make Veggie Supreme Spinach Nachos

Step 1 Arrange the nachos

To prepare this amazing snack, arrange the the Spinach Nacho Crisps on a ceramic plate, overlapping one over the other.

 

Step 2 Pour the salsa sauce over the nachos

Pour the chunky salsa dip slightly on every nacho crisp.

 

Step 3 Add all the veggies on top

Top nacho crisps evenly with all the vegetables and sliced black olives.

 

Step 4 Top with sour cream

Place a scoop of sour cream over the centre and garnish it with coriander and season as per taste. Serve the platter along with cheesy dip.
